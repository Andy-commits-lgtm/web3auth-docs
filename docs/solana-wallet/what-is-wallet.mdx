The Solana Wallet provides a connection to Solana blockchain. Out of the box, the wallet supports OAuth social logins and native biometric logins.

Using wallet means giving your users access to the rest of the Solana ecosystem. Application users can manage their accounts on wallet, head to
[solana.tor.us](https://solana.tor.us) to experience it for yourself.
