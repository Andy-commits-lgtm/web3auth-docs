Managing your billing and analyzing your user statistics is very smooth with Web3Auth, all of it is available in the
[Settings](https://dashboard.web3auth.io/home/settings) section of the dashboard.

You can add a payment method, change your plan, and view your billing history all from the same page. You can also view your user statistics, which
are updated in real-time. Your billing history is also available.
