Managing your billing and analyzing your user statistics is very smooth with Web3Auth, all of it is available in the
[Settings](https://dashboard.web3auth.io/home/settings) section of the dashboard.

The analytics showcase Monthly Active Users(MAU) over various different projects. The billing cycle and credits also show up here. The pricing of
various different plans can be calculated via the [pricing page](https://web3auth.io/pricing.html).

You can add a payment method, change your plan, and view your billing history all from the same page. One can add their credit card seamlessly here.
Billing history provides you will your monthly invoices.
